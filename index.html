home ideas

what:

A lightweight static site covering personal thoughts on low-frequency developments in international securities, commodities and foreign exchange markets. With a major focus on macro, basket trading and the occasional special situation security.

why:

Storing ideas iteratively in a non-volatile and structured manner is incredibly important both for retaining & developing simple thoughts into complex hypotheses. Once the first iteration of the hypothesis is developed, finding holes or further potential goes from being impossible to a complex task; making way for rapid further research by narrowing down the question–(Is this specific oversight in the hypothesis as big or small as it seems?) versus (Is the complete hypothesis valid or not?).

The reasoning above is clear, and it’s why people have been journaling since forever. So why not simply document thoughts & beliefs in a .txt file, in private? Well. The risk of public humiliation & the responsibility of not misleading is a highly effective motivating force to get rid of bias. Second & third, it’s way easier to share this with people and structuring a .txt file isn’t super intuitive.
